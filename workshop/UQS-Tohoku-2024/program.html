Event schedule

  • 8/29 (Thur.): One-day lecture by Chris Greene

         The style and content of the informal talks and discussions are up to the presenter.
         For example, you can introduce your research or raise a question and challenge that you would like to share and discuss
         with other participants. You can use either a slide or blackboard style.
